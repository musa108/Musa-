WELCOME TO BLOCKCHAINUNN

BlockchainUNN is a campus blockchain club created at the University of Nigeria aimed at promoting blockchain adoption within the university environment Founded by three innovative students and key players in the drive for mass adoption

BlockchainUNN educates, impacts and develops members of the university and enthusiasts in general,with proper knowledge of Cryptocurrency and Blockchain, teaching them to how to leverage on blockchain technology in their everyday activities. We also help educate young minds in developing skills like crypto trading, Blockchain Development, and other digital skills like community management, social media, content creation, etc.
